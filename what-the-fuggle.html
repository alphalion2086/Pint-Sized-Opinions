What the Fuggle

Oatmeal Stout

4.9% | 31 IBU

This stout is coffee and chocolate flavored. I could smell both the coffee and chocolate right away before tasting. This Oatmeal Stout was rich and smooth and had a full body taste with a slight caramel touch to it adding a perfect mix of flavors for the finish. This is a great “dessert” drink especially for how filling it is. I wished I asked more about the name! I would definitely recommend this brew.
