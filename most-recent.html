Glass Beach

Amber Ale

5% | 33 IBU

This Amber Ale was described as a classic and traditional Amber. I definitely agree with that statement. The color was the perfect Amber shade and the taste was just classic beer (in a great way) but better. It was a great choice after trying brews with bolder flavors. The mouthfeel was refreshing and it was easy to drink, very smooth, and not too filling. I did not get a specific aftertaste with this beer although the finish was short lived making it so easy to sip on. This Amber was possibly my favorite that I tried from Makai.
