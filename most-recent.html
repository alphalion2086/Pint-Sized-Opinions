Tropical Beer Hug

Goose Island Beer Co.

(Bought in store)

Name and Can Appearance

This IPA comes in a light blue color and a purple shaded beer on the front. The can is bright but not too busy artistically. I love the design of the can as well as the name of “Tropical Beer Hug”. These can come in various sizes from 12oz to 19.2oz.

ABV and Flavor

Before I tried this Imperial IPA, I was a little skeptical. Not for any reason other than, I just started to find IPAs I enjoy. It contains 9.9% ABV and has a Tropical profile with an IBU of 45. I was shocked at the flavor combination and that it wasn't an overpoweringly hoppy taste for me. The flavors consisted of pineapple, mango, and peach. I really enjoyed this Imperial and its refreshing mouthfeel. With the ABV though, I wouldn’t be able to consistently drink it. I would recommend giving this brew a try for not just IPA lovers but everyone.
