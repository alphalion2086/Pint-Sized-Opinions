Sunset Honey

Wheat Ale

4.7% | 27 IBU

This brew is refreshing and light with a slight honey taste. I was slightly surprised by how filling this beer was. The flavor was consistent throughout and came served in a 16oz glass. The aftertaste is more wheat than honey, but it stays light and does not linger. I enjoyed this brew, but I expected a sweeter honey note. The body of the beer was a light orange-yellow, which fits the name well. Overall, I would recommend this to wheat beer lovers and anyone looking for something not too rich or too sweet.
