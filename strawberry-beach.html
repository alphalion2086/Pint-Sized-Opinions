Strawberrie Beach

Blonde

5.3% ABV

I loved the lightness of this beer paired with the strawberry flavor. The strawberry was not too sweet and it added a great touch to the overall flavor. I found this brew easy to sip on and it would be a great option for a summer day or just a casual drink. It sits light making it perfect for a beach beer. I could see this brew being a customer favorite just by the taste and flavor profile not to mention how light it is. Drinking this beer gave me perfect beach day vibes which I assume was the goal. The strawberry is present for the mouthfeel and then turned into a classic Blonde Ale taste for the finish. In all honesty, it got easier to drink after a few sips (and not just because itâ€™s a beer). I would recommend this beer 100% and am certain it would be loved by craft lovers regardless of their preferred style.
